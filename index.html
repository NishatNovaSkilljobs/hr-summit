About The Event

The National HR Summit 2024, themed "Transforming Bangladesh Using AI," is a significant event that will focus on the pivotal role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in revolutionizing human resources and overall business practices within Bangladesh. The summit aims to explore how AI can be leveraged to drive innovation, efficiency, and growth in various sectors, particularly in HR, to keep pace with the global digital transformation.

Key Highlights:

  • 50+ Industry Leaders & HR Experts
  • Keynote Speeches & Panel Discussions
  • Industry-Academia Linkage
  • Networking & Career Opportunities
  • Insight into Emerging HR Trends

Daffodil International University

Daffodil International University (DIU) is a prominent private university located in Savar, Bangladesh. It is known for its modern campus, innovative teaching methods, and diverse academic programs. DIU emphasizes research, entrepreneurship, and technology-driven education, offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fields like engineering, business, and humanities.
